Frequently Asked Questions

Are you an HDB registered renovation contractor?

Yes. Cove Reno is an HDB Registered Renovation Contractor in Singapore and complies with all relevant renovation guidelines.

Do you handle small renovation works?

Yes. No renovation job is too small for us. We take on both minor and larger renovation projects.

Do you provide carpentry services?

Yes. We handle cabinet fabrication, installation, and correction works as part of our renovation services.

Do you handle both design and renovation works?

We focus on renovation execution and practical space planning. Design support is provided where needed depending on project requirements.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Timelines vary depending on scope, but most residential renovations typically take between 6 to 10 weeks.
